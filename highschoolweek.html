FIVE THINGS...

Five Things to Remember During High School Week

  1. Roam around the campus as much as possible.                                                                                                                  - Make sure to visit every booth that there is around the campus. Try out different food, products, and services that these students or sellers offer! Try looking for booths near the Heroes' Park, around the Activity Center, and lastly, inside Carpio Hall.                                                                                                                
  2. Watch the events.                                                                                                                                                                          - Do not forget to see your fellow students give their hearts out during these times. Support those who are competing in these activities. Also, remember to cheer for your respective teams!
  3. Go to school early.                                                                                                                                                                         - Aside from the fact that your team's overall score might get deducted if you arrive in the campus later than 8 o'clock in the morning, be sure to go to school early so that you will not miss any fun-filled activity during these days!
  4. Try competing, too.                                                                                                                                                                        - As they say, there is no harm in trying. Try to join the events that are prepared by the Local Student Council. Who knows, as you get involved in these events, you will not just have great memories, but you might also get to have new friends from other batches or sections.
  5. Take as many pictures as possible.                                                                                                                                         - The Laboratory High School week only lasts for 3 days in a whole year, so do not forget to bring your A-game in taking pictures with your friends (and crushes as well, hehe).
  6. BONUS                                                                                                                                                                                              -Dont forget the spirit of caramaderie. It is innate for a Labhigh student to have a heart that cares for other people, especially to those who belong to the same home that she or he has. Being united is one of the traits that make Labhigh stand apart from other schools, so make sure to always uphold that character of ours! 

    And, that's it. If you got to complete these tips, then you most probably enjoyed your High School Week. See you again next year!
